About Cortisol Daily

Cortisol Daily is a research-driven publication focused on the science of cortisol and its role in stress, sleep, and long-term health. Our coverage centers on cortisol regulation mechanisms, the HPA axis, chronic stress physiology, sleep and circadian interaction with cortisol, and the nutritional and lifestyle factors that influence a balanced stress response.

Each article is structured around current research and reviewed for accuracy before publication. We do not publish anecdotal wellness trends without grounding them in physiological mechanism. If a claim is made about cortisol, a supplement, or a stress-reduction practice, the underlying biological rationale is explained alongside it.

Content focus areas: cortisol regulation and the HPA axis, chronic stress physiology, sleep and circadian rhythm's effect on cortisol, magnesium and mineral interaction with stress hormones, nervous system regulation, and long-term stress management. Sources are drawn from peer-reviewed nutrition and endocrinology research.
